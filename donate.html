Donate a Book to a School

At SCAPS ( Society to Create Awareness on Public Safety) one of our objectives is to develop teaching material on safety education for children. The “Kids Safety Tips” book attached to this note is the first of such publications. The book attempts to create awareness in the child on the safety aspects to be considered in day to day interactions.

Our goal at SCAPS is to reach out to as many Government schools as possible with this publication. Teachers in the Schools would use the book to sensitize children on safety, which would be of lifelong benefit to them.
