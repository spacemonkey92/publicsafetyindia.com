Attention: Educational Institutions & Volunteers

We at SCAPS , in our endeavour to develop teaching and motivating resources on road and general safety, we designed and developed activity based programs to organise at your schools and communities. Concept of these programs are to "Instigate the culture of safety preparedness" in children at home, school and in public places.

We welcome and encourage schools, individuals and colony welfare associations to make use of our resources and conduct this program at your place. You can visit our facebook page , to view our activities. These programs are suitable for children of 10 to 15 years age group.

Contents and design of these programs are copyrights of SCAPS Hydreabad.

Activity Kit



Activity Brief description Get it

"StepOne" an entry level activity on general safety!

Object: Instigate the culture of preparedness measures.
Target Group: Kids of 10years to 15years age.
Takeaways: Learn how to keep "home emergency safety card" update.
Duration: 3hrs

Here with this kit we are providing teaching-learning resources on road and general safety preparedness measures, to teach and motivate kids and teens to follow. While the digital form of these kits are available for free! The physical Kits will be at cost price.

This “StepOne” programme kit is an activity based program material to "Instigate the culture of preparedness measures" in children on road and public safety. Besides detailed notes and video graphic support we provide you in-Phone support to effectively conduct the micro workshop at your school or community.

Activity code:001

Object: Motivation on Pedestrian safety
Target Group:12years to 15 years age
Takeaways:Knowing pedestrian rights and administering of Pledge to follow rules.
Duration: 45 minutes

“WalkRight”,this is to bring about a behavioral change/correction in today's children walking in public space, which is also a part of road safety issue, we come out with a program , “WalkRight” a motivational program on pedestrian safety to educate and understand the rules and the rights to use the public places and roads as a pedestrian and take a oath/ pledge by the school children of 12 to 15 years age group to follow the road rules. This Micro activity kit on pedestrian safety consist of an interactive talk on road and general safety for pedestrian along with short film and self explanatory display posters on pedestrian rules.

We welcome and encourage schools, individuals and colony welfare associations to make use of our resources to conduct this program at your place. Besides detailed notes and video graphic support we provide you in-Phone support to effectively conduct this activity in your school or community.
